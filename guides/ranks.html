Ranks

Grub

Every player begins their journey on CrispyMiners as a Grub. The Grub is the lowest of the low, the weakest of the weak, the rank that every player wants to desperately get past.

Grub Goose

Grub Goose is one solid step up from being a Grub. You may not be the best of the best, but you've clearly devoted at least a couple hours of time on CrispyMiners!

Rankup requirements:

  • Play for 2 hours
  • Kill 20 mobs
  • McRPG Woodcutting level of 20
  • McRPG mining level of 50
  • Mine 20 coal ore
  • Mine 20 iron ore
  • Mine 500 stone
  • Craft a white bed

Rankup rewards:

  • Armor Stand Editor perms
  • CoreProtect inspect perms
  • Brewery perms
  • 1 extra home

Goose

Goose is the average rank. Not too difficult to get to but it'll sure have ya honkin!

Rankup requirements:

  • Play for 12 hours
  • Kill 100 mobs
  • Kill 10 creepers
  • Kill 5 witches
  • Kill 20 zombies
  • McRPG swords level of 50
  • McRPG excavation level of 50
  • XP level of 20
  • Craft a clock

Rankup rewards:

  • Wild teleport perms
  • Shopkeeper perms

Greater Goose

To become a Greater Goose requires time, dedication, and a love for CrispyMiners. By now you'll likely have a well established base and be a well known player.

Rankup requirements:

  • Play for 24 hours
  • Kill 250 mobs
  • Kill 15 husks
  • Kill 50 skeletons
  • Kill 10 slimes
  • McRPG power level of 500
  • McRPG axes level of 50
  • McRPG fishing level of 50
  • XP level of 30
  • Craft a compass

Rankup rewards:

  • Heads perms
  • One extra home

Golden Goose

As a Golden Goose, you are truly elite among geese. The gold plates your feathers and shines in the morning sun.

Rankup requirements:

  • Play for 48 hours
  • Kill 500 mobs
  • Kill 15 drowned
  • Kill 20 wither skeletons
  • Kill 10 ghasts
  • McRPG power level of 1000
  • McRPG archery level of 75
  • McRPG herbalism level of 75
  • Mine 10,000 stone

Rankup rewards:

  • /workbench perms
  • Color codes on signs perms

Gilsoun Goose

The Gilsoun Goose is a god among geese. Named after our lord and savior, Gilsoun himself, the Gilsoun Goose as the highest of the goose ranks. It takes a lot to be a Gilsoun Goose but it is well worth it.

Rankup requirements:

  • Play for 72 hours
  • Kill 750 mobs
  • Kill 30 pillagers
  • Kill 20 phantoms
  • Kill 50 cave spiders
  • McRPG power level of 2500
  • McRPG fitness level of 100
    • McRPG sorcery level of 100
  • XP level of 40
  • Obtain a nether star

Rankup rewards:

  • /hat perms
  • Editable signs perms
  • One extra home

Chungus

So you've surpassed all of the goose ranks? Well you're not done yet! Chungus is the first of the elite ranks, named after the false prophet Big Chungus.

Rankup requirements:

  • Play for 100 hours
  • Kill 1000 mobs
  • Kill 30 vindicators
  • Kill 100 endermen
  • Kill 20 shulkers
  • McRPG power level of 5000
  • McRPG fitness level of 500
  • McRPG archery level of 500
  • McRPG excavation level of 500
  • Craft 5 conduits

Rankup rewards:

  • /echest perms

  • Play for 168 hours
  • Kill 1500 mobs

  • Kill 15 elder guardians
  • Kill 5 ravagers
  • McRPG axes level of 500
